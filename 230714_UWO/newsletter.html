Seven Wonders Chapter 3.9:
The Silk Road has arrived!
Attention all Captains!

From a new Legacy Theme centered around trades and routes to exciting new improvements introduced to the game, there's a lot to discover and experience in this update!
New Legacy
A new Legacy Theme has arrived, centered around trades and routes! Journey across the world in search of the ancient trade route's legacy!
New Buildable Ships
Chapter 3.9 introduces four new galley ships for you to build, offering exciting construction options for your maritime endeavors.
1 Billion Check & Increase in
Carriable Ducats
A 1 Billion Check has been added for smoother transactions. Additionally, the amount of Ducats you can carry on your Character has increased from 300 million to 500 million Ducats!
