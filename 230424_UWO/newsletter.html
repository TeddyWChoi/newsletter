Seven Wonders Chapter 3.7
REGALIA is here!
In this new Chapter update, we are introducing a better
balance for existing buildable Ships!
A new Legacy Theme featuring exquisite items from
around the world has also been added.
Buffs on Existing Ships
Some of our in-game buildable Ships have been
given a major buff to improve the game balance!
You'll notice improvements in Durability, Armour,
Turn Speed, Cabin, Cannon Chamber, and/or
required Battle level of some ships.
Company Shop Search Now Available
Now you can search Company Shops for items
you want to buy!
This search function is linked to the database of all
UWO items, so it's also useful to check out the
lineup of existing items in our Maris Server.
New Legacy Theme
With this update, a brand new addition has been made
to the "Seven Wonders" Legacies.
Complete a specific Quest and use what you discover
to obtain this sparkling Legacy Theme!
