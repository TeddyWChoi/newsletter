WarRock 18th Anniversary Gifting.
Dear Loyal soldiers of WarRock.

We would like to celebrate the 18th birthday of WarRock by giving out permanent Jersey character parts to everyone.

During the next 2 weeks, from May 10th to May 24th, log in to WarRock more than 3 times.

Please note that play-time will ONLY be counted when you complete a match. (Match results screen must be seen.)
Don't miss out on this one and only chance to collect Anniversary18th Jerseys for your characters permanently!
*Jerseys can only be used with certain characters.
See you in the game, soliders!
